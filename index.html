Working group on the SWHID identifier

SWHID Working Group Homepage

The SWHID Working Group oversees development of the SWHID materials in the SWHID GitHub org, including the following:

  • the SWHID Specification itself
  • the various software libraries and tools provided by the SWHID project

Participation and governance

Participation in the elaboration of the SWHID standard is open to all, see the dedicated SWHID Specification repository for information on how to join. The working group follows the principles stated in the governance repository.
